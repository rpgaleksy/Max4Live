-Input and Output Converters for the Launchpad X Programmer Mode-
	made by rpg.aleksy


Input Converter:

"CCs -> Notes" converts input from the Launchpad X's side and top buttons from CC messages to notes corresponding to the Launchpad Pro's outer buttons.
The settings at the bottom set which of the Pro's side buttons the top row on the X will emulate.


Output Converter:

Simply match the settings at the bottom to the ones on the Input Converter and your lights will output as they should.
